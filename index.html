About Me

Regulatory Affairs Specialist Pharmacist

I am a dedicated professional with a Master of Science in Regulatory Affairs of Drugs, Biologics, and Devices from Northeastern University. My career is defined by a commitment to improving healthcare standards through rigorous regulatory practices.

Currently serving as a Consultant for the World Health Organization (WHO) Prequalification of Medicines Team, I assess pharmaceutical products and review global guidelines. Previously, I served as Manager of Harmonization at the National Drug Authority of Uganda.

Health & Tech

With extensive experience in the East African Community (EAC) Medicines Regulation Harmonization Project, I understand the power of systems and standards.

I am now expanding my skillset into Generative AI to explore how large language models and automation can enhance regulatory efficiency, data analysis, and scientific communication.
